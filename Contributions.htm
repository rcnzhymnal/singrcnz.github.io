Contributions

We have had a number of submissions and these have been very valuable.  Some have suggested just one song, while others have pointed us to a number of songs.  A number of people have helpfully provided feedback in the form of corrections, complaints or recommendations of specific wordings or music.  Such specific feedback has been valuable to us.

One very easy way for you to contribute is to help get the word around by emailing a link to this website to your church friends. Click mailto:friend

Besides personal submissions to the committee, below are some other significant ways you can contribute.

Contribute by Careful Selection

Another way to contribute would be to get together in a group with others to carefully select songs from a wider variety of sources than the ones we already sing from.

For more information, see the contact list.
