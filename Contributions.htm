Contributions

 

We have had a number of submissions and these have been very valuable.  Some have suggested just one song, while others have pointed us to a number of songs.  A number of people have helpfully provided feedback in the form of corrections, complaints or recommendations of specific wordings or music.  Such specific feedback has been valuable to us.

 

One very easy way for you to contribute is to help get the word around by emailing a link to this website to your church friends. Click mailto:friend

 

Besides personal submissions to the committee, below are some other significant ways you can contribute.

Contribute Statistics

A valuable contribution was submitted by one of our sessions which asked their church members to list songs they would like to keep from our current hymnbook and from other approved sources.  They provided us with a summary of the responses.  If you could organize such submissions from your own church it would be invaluable.

 

We would like you to help us make this a denominational hymnbook.  If you can provide us with lists of songs and how many want each song, that would be helpful.  A form for reporting this information is available here. 

 

As an example, you can read one congregation’s plan of how to do this here.

Contribute by Careful Selection

Another way to contribute would be to get together in a group with others to carefully select songs from a wider variety of sources than the ones we already sing.
