Name the Psalter Hymnal

Name our New Zealand Psalter Hymnal. Simply send your suggestions to Berwyn Hoyt by November 2007. Names submitted so far are:


  • The New Zealand Psalter Hymnal

  • Songs of Praise


We're about to go to print! – And in two flavours: both hard cover and 'soft'.  The committee preparing our new Psalter hymnal is almost ready to release the first section. This will be the entire book of Psalms released on CD. This is the 'soft' version, and much of it has also been recently released on our hymns page.


But the Reformed Church of Dovedale is also taking the initiative of publishing this 'soft' version in a real, high quality, hard-cover book as a provisional work.  Now that is news worth talking about.

But the book needs a name, and it would be nice to have the final name even on this early edition.  The default name is New Zealand Psalter Hymnal, and it is appropriate. But that's where you come in – to provide a selection of name options for the final hymnal.
