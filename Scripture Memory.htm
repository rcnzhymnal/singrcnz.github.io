Scripture Memory

The following features of the Scripture sections of our new hymnal are intended to make memorisation of Scripture easier by:

  • Numbering songs by Psalm number: 1a, 1b, 2a, ...
  • Using words that follow Scripture more closely while maintaining the poetry.
  • Updating the language.
  • Retaining the good tunes from our current hymnal and replacing the poor ones.
  • Providing one main tune for each Psalm, together with a summary psalm or alternate tune where appropriate.

This helps to associate one specific tune with each Psalm, which aids our memory of the Psalms and other Scripture — especially with more accurate translations.

It is hoped this will become a valuable resource for teaching our children the Scriptures. (For example, some Christian schools have used songs as a means of easy Scripture memorization.)
