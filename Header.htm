Viewing:          The first time you click a view/play link, you will be asked to download a browser plug-in called Scorch to play the file.  This plug-in will let you play and sing-along while you view the file.

 

 Note: Because of recent changes you may get an error message “The score did not load successfully”.  If you get that message, follow the procedure here.

 

Proofing:         In contrast to what was previously published here, we have now decided to publish only songs that have been fully proofed and corrected.  Note that there may still be timing irregularities in the computer play-back as we have not polished that up yet.

 

Parts:              We have included parts to help the choir-singers play-along and learn their part.  Just click on the correct SATB icon before the song name (SATB stands for Soprano, Alto, Tenor Bass).  Note that these parts are auto-generated from the full-part score, and may contain errors where there is complicated harmony.  We do not intend to fix these.

 

Updates:         Please take the time to browse the songs below.  This page will be updated regularly as we progress through the project.  Click this link if you wish to provide feedback.
