Psalm Projection Slides

Almost all of the Psalm slides may be used by the Reformed Churches of New Zealand free of charge: we have either been given permission or, for a few, we have paid for it. You can download the Psalm slides here. But note that we have not been able to obtain permission to project Psalms 57b or 139:1-3 at all.

Only churches with CCLI licenses can project Psalm 57b, and no-one may project Psalm 139:1-3. For churches in the RCNZ, these are the only remaining Psalms with projection limitations.

Note that a number of these do require special notes to be placed on the slides as we have done. If you make up your own slides, then you will also have to place these notes. Here is the full list of songs and special notes.

If you are not in the Reformed Churches of New Zealand then you will have to obtain your own permission if you wish to project any of the Psalms listed here.
