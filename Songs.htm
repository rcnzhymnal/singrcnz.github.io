Songs Done so Far

Copyright: This music is copyright and may not be copied except according to these details. Note, however, that publication permission for the Sing to the Lord (provisional) psalter has already been published and anything may be freely sung from that publication.

Projection: Most of the songs may be projected for public singing. We hope to be making powerpoint slides of all the Psalms available by October 2008. However, a few of these are subject to this special handling.

Viewing: The first time you click a view/play link, you will be asked to download a browser plug-in called Scorch to play the file. This plug-in will let you play and sing-along while you view the file. Note: If you get the message "The score did not load successfully", follow the procedure here.

Parts: We may have included parts to help the choir-singers play-along and learn their part. Just click on the correct SATB icon before the song name (SATB stands for Soprano, Alto, Tenor Bass). Note that these parts are auto-generated from the full-part score, and may contain errors where there is complicated harmony. We do not intend to fix these.

Updates: These song pages will be updated regularly as we progress through the project. Click this link if you wish to provide feedback.
