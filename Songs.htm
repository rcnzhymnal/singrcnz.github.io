Song Information

Copyright: The Psalms and hymns available here are copyright and may not be copied except according to these details. Note, however, that publication permission for the Sing to the Lord (provisional) psalter has already been obtained and anything may be freely sung from that publication.

Projection: Most of the Psalms may be projected for public singing. We hope to be making powerpoint slides of all the songs. However, some are subject to this special handling and copyright restrictions.

Viewing: The first time you click a view/play link, the bottom of the page will ask you to download a browser plug-in called Scorch to play the file. This plug-in will let you play and sing-along while you view the file. Note: If you have the CD that came with the Sing to the Lord (provisional) book, then you will find it easier to install Scorch from there.

Parts: For some of the Psalms we have included parts that choir singers can play to help learn their part. Just click on the correct SATB icon before the song name (SATB stands for Soprano, Alto, Tenor Bass). Note that these parts are auto-generated from the full-part score, and may contain errors where there is complicated harmony. We do not intend to fix these.

Updates: These song pages will be updated regularly as we progress through the project. Click this link if you wish to provide feedback.
