Projection of Songs for Public Singing

Although some powerpoint slides have been withheld from the public site, if you contact us we may be able, under certain conditions, to give you the entire book of slides in various formats (Powerpoint/MediaShout, SongShow Plus).

Note, however, that for many of the slides that we send out, your church will need to abide by certain copyright criteria in order to project them for singing.
